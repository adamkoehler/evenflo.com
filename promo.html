Finding the Right Flange Fit

In order to have your pump function optimally, your ﬂange must ﬁt properly.

Note: When using an insert, use only one soft ﬂange insert per ﬂange. Flange size ﬁt may vary between left and right breast.

  • Correct ﬂange size.

    While pumping, your nipple should move freely in the breast ﬂange tunnel. You should see space around the nipple. A minimal amount of nipple. A minimal amount of your areola is drawn into the tunnel with the nipple.

  • Flange too small.

    While pumping, some or all of your nipple rubs against the sides of the breast ﬂange tunnel. Use a larger size.

  • Flange too large.

    While pumping, more areola is drawn into the breast ﬂange with your nipple. Your areola may rub against the side of the breast ﬂange tunnel. Use a smaller size.
