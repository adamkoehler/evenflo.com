Balance Plus

HomeBalance Plus Bottles

Your bond is strong. Your baby’s latch should be too.

Feeding your baby is one of the most important bonding expereinces you can have with them. That’s why we’ve created the Balance + Wide Neck Bottle. It’s uniquely designed to help support healthy bottle-feeding without interfering with your personal breastfeeding journey. We developed a unique and intentional nipple shape that allows for a proper lathc and adjusted flow rate that promotes a comfortable drinking pace.

  • Premium Venting

    Features our patented ProfloTM Venting Technology. Designed to reduce the potential for colic, reflux, gas and fussiness.

  • Unique Nipple Shape

    Engages baby's full mouth and provides a resting area for baby's lips to flange and properly latch.

  • Gentle Slope

    Encourages baby to maintain a wide latch, without sliding to the tip, key for breastfed babies.

  • Just Right Nipple Length

    Stimulates the soft palate which triggers baby's natural sucking reflex.

  • FloRightTM

    Adjusted, slower flow rate allows babies to enjoy more comfortable, gulp-free feeding. No need for baby to gulp to keep up.
